"To be used for general information only. Data is highly generalized and was digitized from hand drawn maps...

...Data originally compiled and drawn manually (pen and ink) sometime in the late '70s. They were first digitized in 1983 or 1984. The data source can be traced to individual Wildlife Managers based throughout Arizona. Initial editing was performed by Phoenix-based Arizona Game and Fish Dept. biologists, and final editing was performed by Regional AGFD biologists (e.g.Tucson, Kingman, Flagstaff, etc.). "
